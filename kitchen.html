Kitchen Rules & Dish Options

~1800 cal · 90g protein

⚠️ Kitchen Rules

🛢Oil: 1 level tbsp (15ml) MEASURED per dish. Mustard / ghee only. No refined. No reheating oil.
🌾Atta: Multigrain (wheat+chana+bajra). No maida ever.
🚫Never: Zero sugar in tea/food/drinks. No jaggery. No cream/malai.
🥛Make daily: Curd at home. Chaas (curd+water+jeera+pudina+kala namak). Haldi doodh at night.
📦Every night: Soak 5 almonds + 2 walnuts. Pack dabba. Fill 750ml bottle.
